## Transformations
- #### Vertical Translation
	- $f(x)=(x)+c,(c=\uparrow )$
	- $f(x)=(x)-c,(c=\downarrow )$
- #### Horizontal Translation
	- $f(x)=(x-c),(c=\to )$
	- $f(x)=(x+c),(c= \leftarrow  )$
- #### Vertical Stretch 
	- $(a,cf(x) \to, (c= \updownarrow))$
